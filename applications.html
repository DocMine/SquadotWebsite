Matching Logic

匹配逻辑

Four inputs for platform fit

四项输入确定平台适配

Depth range 水深范围

Depth, hovering and operating duration quickly separate platform families.

水深、悬停与作业时长会快速区分平台系列。

Space boundary 空间边界

Open water, culverts, shafts and gate slots require different access logic.

开阔水域、涵洞、井室与闸槽对应不同进入逻辑。

Observation demand 观测需求

Routine review, precision observation and experiment support require different stability levels.

常规复核、精细观测与试验支持需要不同稳定性等级。

Payload and delivery 挂载与交付

Some missions need only video; others require sonar, sensing, payloads or field support.

有些任务只需视频，有些需要声呐、传感、挂载或现场支持。

Scene 01

场景 01

Ports and nearshore assets

港口与近岸资产

Low visibility, piles, cables, sediment or path-support needs move the task beyond basic video review.

低能见度、桩基、缆线、淤积或路径辅助需求会超出基础视频复核。

Client problem 客户问题

Single-view observation is often insufficient in structure-rich waters.

结构密集水域中，单视角观测通常不足以支撑判断。

Recommended platform 推荐平台

SeaLeopard, with sonar, DVL or environmental sensing when required.

SeaLeopard，可按需配置声呐、DVL 或环境感知模块。

Delivery: platform, sensing module and mission support. Product: SeaLeopard.

交付：平台、感知模块与任务支持。产品：SeaLeopard。

Scene 02

场景 02

Dams and hydraulic assets

大坝与水工资产

These tasks require stable close viewing, depth hold and condition confirmation without diver-first deployment.

任务需要稳定近距观察、定深控制与状态确认，减少对潜水员先行进入的依赖。

Client problem 客户问题

Operators need clear target-surface imagery while holding a stable viewing distance.

操作者需要在稳定观察距离下获得清晰目标表面图像。

Recommended platform 推荐平台

Challenge for routine work-class review; SeaLeopard where more payload or stability is required.

常规工作级复核采用 Challenge；需要更高挂载或稳定性时采用 SeaLeopard。

Delivery: purchase or mission support, depending on task frequency.

交付：按任务频次选择采购或任务支持。

Scene 03

场景 03

Precision observation and underwater tests

精密观测与水下试验

These missions require fine attitude control, repeatable response and stable target alignment.

任务需要精细姿态控制、可重复响应与稳定目标对准。

Client problem 客户问题

Observation quality drops quickly when posture stability is insufficient.

姿态稳定性不足时，观测质量会迅速下降。

Recommended platform 推荐平台

Hammerhead with multi-axis control and task-specific support frames.

Hammerhead，配置多轴控制与任务专用支撑结构。

Delivery: rental, test support or project expansion around the experiment setup.

交付：围绕试验装置提供租赁、测试支持或项目扩展。

Scene 04

场景 04

Shallow pilot checks and tanks

浅水先导检查与池体

Fast deployment and low operating threshold matter more than heavy payload capacity.

快速部署和低操作门槛比重型挂载能力更重要。

Client problem 客户问题

Large platforms are often unnecessary in ponds, pits and trial tanks.

池塘、基坑和试验水槽中，大型平台往往没有必要。

Recommended platform 推荐平台

Inspire for quick entry and shallow-water review.

Inspire，用于快速进入与浅水复核。

Delivery: rental or short mission support for pilot judgement and validation.

交付：租赁或短期任务支持，用于先导判断与验证。

Scene 05

场景 05

Confined industrial spaces

受限工业空间

Culverts, gate slots, shafts, box channels and tanks prioritize compact entry over broad-water mobility.

涵洞、闸槽、井室、箱涵与罐体优先考虑紧凑进入，而非开阔水域机动性。

Client problem 客户问题

Large platforms cannot enter reliably, but anomaly confirmation is still required.

大型平台难以可靠进入，但仍需完成异常确认。

Recommended platform 推荐平台

Linglong first; Inspire when the space is less constrained and the task remains shallow.

优先选择 Linglong；空间较宽且任务仍属浅水时可选 Inspire。

Delivery: portable inspection support, shallow anomaly review and light module expansion.

交付：便携巡检支持、浅水异常复核与轻量模块扩展。
