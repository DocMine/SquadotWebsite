We Are Squadot Tec.

我们是谁

Inspection and observation systems

巡检与观测平台体系

Squadot Tec. is a productization-oriented engineering team providing micro to medium underwater robotics platforms for underwater observation, structural inspection, constrained-space review, shallow-water checks and project-specific payload growth.

北京方点科技是一支具备产品化能力的工程团队，围绕水下观测、结构巡检、狭窄空间检查、浅水摸查 和项目化增配需求，提供微型到中型水下机器人平台与配套服务。

Current delivery modes include product purchase, equipment rental, field mission support and payload-oriented integration.

当前可提供产品采购、设备租赁、现场任务支持和围绕传感器、支架与专项模块的项目化集成服务。

Applications Snapshot

场景摘要

Three primary application directions

This is the fastest map from task type to platform family and delivery direction

Hydraulic Inspection 水工巡检

Dams and hydraulic assets

坝体与水工设施

For close structural review without sending divers first. Recommended platforms: Challenge and SeaLeopard.

适合在不先组织潜水作业的情况下进行近距结构复核。推荐平台：破阵、海豹。

Complex Waters 复杂水域

Ports and nearshore structures

港口与近岸结构

For lower visibility, structure-rich waters and sensing-ready payload packages. Recommended platform: SeaLeopard.

适合低能见度、结构复杂且需要传感挂载的水域。推荐平台：海豹。

Constrained Access 受限空间

Culverts, shafts and tanks

涵洞、井室与池体

When larger platforms cannot enter but visual confirmation is still required. Recommended platforms: Linglong and Inspire.

当大平台无法进入但仍需完成可视化复核时使用。推荐平台：玲珑、启艋。
