Applications Snapshot

场景摘要

Three mission directions

三类任务方向

Map task type to platform family and delivery mode

将任务类型映射到平台系列和交付方式

Hydraulic Inspection 水工巡检

Dams and hydraulic assets

大坝与水工资产

Close structural review without diver-first deployment. Platforms: Challenge, SeaLeopard.

无需潜水员先行进入，即可完成近距结构复核。平台：Challenge、SeaLeopard。

Complex Waters 复杂水域

Ports and nearshore structures

港口与近岸结构

Low-visibility or structure-rich waters requiring sensor-ready payloads. Platform: SeaLeopard.

低能见度或结构密集水域，需要可扩展传感挂载。平台：SeaLeopard。

Constrained Access 受限进入

Culverts, shafts and tanks

涵洞、井室与罐体

Visual confirmation where larger platforms cannot enter. Platforms: Linglong, Inspire.

大型平台无法进入时完成视觉确认。平台：Linglong、Inspire。
