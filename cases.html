Case 01

案例 01

Nearshore inspection

近岸结构巡检

For structure-rich waters where path support, environment awareness and collision-risk reduction matter.

面向结构密集水域，重点关注路径辅助、环境感知与碰撞风险降低。

Client problem 客户问题

Piles, cables, sediment and low visibility make single-mode observation insufficient.

桩基、缆线、淤积和低能见度使单一观测模式不足。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

SeaLeopard with scanning sonar, DVL and environmental sensing when required.

SeaLeopard，可按需配置扫描声呐、DVL 与环境感知。

Value: stronger environment awareness and path-support logic. Product: SeaLeopard.

价值：增强环境感知与路径辅助逻辑。产品：SeaLeopard。

Case 02

案例 02

Precision observation

精密观测

Focused on precise operator response, controlled posture and repeatable target alignment.

聚焦精确操作响应、受控姿态与可重复目标对准。

Client problem 客户问题

Observation quality depends directly on posture stability and angle control.

观测质量直接依赖姿态稳定和角度控制。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Hammerhead with multi-axis motion control and task-specific support frames.

Hammerhead，配置多轴运动控制与任务专用支撑结构。

Value: better posture stability and execution precision. Product: Hammerhead.

价值：提升姿态稳定性与执行精度。产品：Hammerhead。

Case 03

案例 03

Hydraulic inspection

水工巡检

Routine structural review where close-range imaging and stable hovering matter more than payload complexity.

常规结构复核，近距成像和稳定悬停比复杂挂载更重要。

Client problem 客户问题

The structure must be inspected without organizing diver-first access.

需要在不组织潜水员先行进入的情况下完成结构检查。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Challenge with HD visual observation, depth hold and ground-station monitoring.

Challenge，提供高清视觉观测、定深与地面站监控。

Value: reviewable structure records for follow-up maintenance. Product: Challenge.

价值：形成可复核的结构记录，支持后续维护。产品：Challenge。

Case 04

案例 04

Shallow pilot checks

浅水先导检查

For quick judgement, close review and fast entry where small deployable platforms are more efficient.

面向快速判断、近距复核和快速入场，小型可部署平台更高效。

Client problem 客户问题

The work is short-cycle and does not justify larger equipment deployment.

任务周期短，不适合调动大型装备。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Inspire with fast deployment, network communication and six-thruster shallow-water control.

Inspire，具备快速部署、网络通信与六推进器浅水控制。

Value: fast first-pass judgement and low-threshold field review. Product: Inspire.

价值：快速形成第一轮判断，降低现场复核门槛。产品：Inspire。

Case 05

案例 05

Confined-space inspection

受限空间巡检

When culverts, shafts, gate slots or box channels make entry size the main selection factor.

当涵洞、井室、闸槽或箱涵让入口尺寸成为主要选型因素。

Client problem 客户问题

Large platforms cannot enter, but local anomaly review and record keeping still have to be completed.

大型平台无法进入，但仍需完成局部异常复核和记录。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Linglong with compact body, shallow-water video review and rapid deployment.

Linglong，具备紧凑机身、浅水视频复核与快速部署。

Value: lower entry threshold and faster anomaly confirmation. Product: Linglong.

价值：降低进入门槛，加快异常确认。产品：Linglong。
