Case 01

案例 01

Port and nearshore complex-environment mapping support

港口与近岸复杂环境测绘支持

This case direction targets structure-rich waters where path-support, environment awareness and reduced collision risk matter.

该类任务面向结构密集水域，重点在于路径辅助、环境感知和降低碰撞风险。

Client problem 客户问题

Piles, cables, sediment and lower visibility make single-mode observation insufficient.

桩基、缆线、沉积物和低能见度使单一观测方式难以满足任务要求。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

SeaLeopard with scanning sonar, DVL and environment sensing growth direction.

海豹平台，并按项目引入扫描声呐、DVL 和环境感知方向。

Value delivered: improved environment awareness and better path-support logic in complex waters. Related product: SeaLeopard.

客户价值：提升复杂水域环境感知能力与路径辅助效率。对应产品：海豹。

Case 02

案例 02

High-demand attitude control and underwater experiment support

高要求姿态控制与水下实验支持

The key value here is precise operator response, controlled posture and repeatable target alignment.

这一类任务的关键价值在于精确操控响应、可控姿态和可重复的目标对准能力。

Client problem 客户问题

Observation quality depends directly on posture stability and angle control.

观测质量直接受姿态稳定性和角度控制能力影响。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Hammerhead with multi-axis motion control and task-specific support frames.

锤头鲨平台配合多轴运动控制和专项任务支架能力。

Value delivered: stronger posture stability and better execution precision for demanding observation tasks. Related product: Hammerhead.

客户价值：提升高要求观测任务中的姿态稳定性与执行精度。对应产品：锤头鲨。

Case 03

案例 03

Small dam underwater inspection and maintenance support

小型坝体水下检测与维护

This direction focuses on routine structural review where clear close-range imaging and stable hovering matter more than broad payload complexity.

这一类任务重点在于稳定的近距图像获取和悬停观察，而不是复杂的多挂载系统。

Client problem 客户问题

The structure must be inspected without organizing diver-first access.

需要在不以潜水作业为前提的情况下完成关键结构复核。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Challenge with HD visual observation, depth hold and ground-station monitoring.

破阵平台配合高清视觉观测、定深悬停和地面站监看能力。

Value delivered: usable structure state records and follow-up review support in small hydraulic environments. Related product: Challenge.

客户价值：形成可复核的结构状态记录，为后续检查与维护提供支持。对应产品：破阵。

Case 04

案例 04

Shallow-water pilot observation and trial-pool review

浅水先导观察与试验池排查

The site only needs quick judgement, close review and fast entry, so smaller deployable platforms become more efficient.

现场更需要快速判断、近距复核和快速下水，因此可快速部署的小平台更高效。

Client problem 客户问题

The work is short-cycle and does not justify larger equipment deployment.

任务周期短，不适合启用更大体量的平台与部署成本。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Inspire with fast deployment logic, network communication and six-thruster shallow-water control.

启艋平台配合快速部署、网络通信和六推浅水控制逻辑。

Value delivered: rapid first-pass judgement and low-threshold field review in shallow-water scenarios. Related product: Inspire.

客户价值：在浅水任务中快速形成第一轮现场判断并降低部署门槛。对应产品：启艋。

Case 05

案例 05

Constrained industrial-space observation

狭窄工业场景观测

When access is restricted by culverts, shafts, gate slots or box channels, entry size becomes the main product-selection factor.

当现场受限于涵洞、井室、闸门槽或箱涵时，入口尺寸就成为首要选型因素。

Client problem 客户问题

Large platforms cannot enter, but local anomaly review and record keeping still have to be completed.

大型平台难以进入，但局部异常点复核和记录仍然必须完成。

Platform / capability 平台 / 能力

Linglong with compact body, shallow-water video review and rapid deployment logic.

玲珑平台配合紧凑机身、浅水视频观测和快速部署逻辑。

Value delivered: lower entry threshold for constrained inspection scenes and faster anomaly confirmation. Related product: Linglong.

客户价值：降低狭窄场景的进入门槛并提升异常点确认效率。对应产品：玲珑。
