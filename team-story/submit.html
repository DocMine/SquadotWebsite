Submission Scope投稿范围

Useful material for a reliable team archive可靠团队档案需要的材料

Prioritize original files, names, dates and context. The archive should stay traceable rather than becoming a loose memory wall.优先提交原始文件、姓名、日期和背景说明。档案应保持可追溯，而不是松散回忆墙。

  • Team photos by year, with member names and photographer permission.按年份整理的团队照片，并附成员姓名和摄影授权说明。
  • Award certificates, competition notices, team rosters and project names.获奖证书、赛事通知、参赛名单和项目名称。
  • Robot build logs, technical reports, CAD snapshots and field-test records.机器人搭建日志、技术报告、CAD 截图和外场测试记录。
  • Corrections to public-source text, especially year, award name and team-member details.对公开资料初稿的更正，尤其是年份、奖项名称和成员信息。
