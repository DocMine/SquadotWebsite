A student team built around underwater systems 围绕水下系统成长的学生团队

The current text is a first-pass public archive. It should be replaced with verified internal history, member lists and original photos when the operating team provides them. 当前文字是公开资料初稿。待社团运营团队提供正式资料后，可替换为完整队史、成员名单与原始照片。

G_Robot is a student robotics community associated with Beijing Information Science and Technology University. Public articles describe the club as established in 2014, open to students across grades and majors, and organized around electronics, control, communication, mechanical design and water-robot projects. G_Robot 是北京信息科技大学学生机器人社团。公开报道显示，社团自 2014 年成立以来，面向不同年级和专业开放，长期围绕电子、控制、通信、机械设计与水下机器人项目组织训练。

Its story is not a single award list. The more useful archive thread is how students moved from early ROV development into repeated underwater robot competitions, then used competitions, reports and outreach activities to keep the technical ladder alive. 它不只是一个获奖清单。更值得作为首页叙事的是：学生团队如何从早期 ROV 研制出发，通过水下机器人赛事、技术文档和科普活动，把技术梯队持续传下去。

Development History发展史

From club formation to competition archive 从社团成立到竞赛档案

The timeline below is summarized from public reports and technical documents, with conservative wording where records are incomplete. 下方时间线来自公开报道和技术文档，未能完整核验的部分采用保守表述。

2014

Club formation社团成立

Public reporting traces the club back to 2014, when G_Robot began building a student technical community around robotics and competition projects.公开报道将社团源头追溯至 2014 年，G_Robot 由此开始围绕机器人与竞赛项目建立学生技术共同体。

2016

Observation ROV development观察级 ROV 研制

Later features describe the team as starting observation-class ROV development in 2016, forming several underwater robot iterations through repeated design and testing.后续报道提到社团自 2016 年开始进行观察级 ROV 水下机器人研制，并在持续设计、测试中形成多代水下机器人作品。

2019

Underwater competition breakthrough水下竞赛突破

A 2021 feature recalls a previous summer when the team joined major underwater robot competitions and formed the memory of an underwater double-title run.2021 年专题文章回顾了团队此前暑期参加重要水下机器人赛事，并形成“水下双冠”的团队记忆。

2022

International technical documentation国际赛事技术文档

The BISTU G_Robot team published a 2022 MATE ROV technical documentation package, showing a more formal engineering-report workflow.BISTU G_Robot 团队公开了 2022 年 MATE ROV 技术文档，呈现出更正式的工程报告和国际赛事表达方式。

2023

Award-dense underwater robot season水下赛事集中获奖

BISTU School of Automation reported that G_Robot won multiple prizes at the 2023 International Underwater Robot Competition, including first-prize results in underwater operation and target grasping events.北京信息科技大学自动化学院报道，G_Robot 在 2023 国际水中机器人大赛中取得多项成绩，其中包括水下作业赛和目标抓取项目中的一等奖成绩。

2024

Awards plus outreach竞赛成果与科普活动

Public records in 2024 show both competition results and outreach activities, indicating the team was still maintaining student recruitment, technical practice and social-facing communication.2024 年公开资料同时出现竞赛获奖和科普活动记录，说明团队仍在维持学生招新、技术训练与面向社会的传播工作。
