2023

International Underwater Robot Competition results国际水中机器人大赛成果

BISTU School of Automation reported that G_Robot won four first prizes, five second prizes and four third prizes, including first-prize results tied to underwater operation and target grasping events.北京信息科技大学自动化学院报道，G_Robot 获得一等奖 4 项、二等奖 5 项、三等奖 4 项，其中包含水下作业赛冠军一等奖和目标抓取项目亚军一等奖等成绩。

2024

China Robot Competition and advanced robotics events中国机器人大赛与先进机器人赛事
