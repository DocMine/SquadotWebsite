Support Logic

支持逻辑

Standard first, mission growth second

先标准平台，再任务扩展

Most projects should start with a deliverable standard platform, then expand only where sensing, frames or delivery mode require it.

多数项目应先从可交付标准平台开始，只在传感、结构或交付方式需要时扩展。

Standard platform 标准平台

Use the current direct-delivery platform as the first matching base.

以当前可直接交付的平台作为第一匹配基础。

Module growth 模块扩展

Add sensing, lighting, sampling or support frames only where the mission needs them.

仅在任务需要时增加感知、照明、采样或支撑结构。

Delivery alignment 交付匹配

Purchase, rental or mission support should follow task cycle and field intensity.

采购、租赁或任务支持应跟随任务周期与现场强度。

First platform recommendation

Share depth range, entry size and payload direction to narrow the platform path.

提供水深范围、入口尺寸与挂载方向，用于收敛平台路径。
