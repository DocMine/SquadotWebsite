Engineering background

Team background spans acoustics, underwater robotics, navigation, SLAM and underwater system design.

Platform engineering

Perception and navigation

System integration

Company Approach

公司方法

Standard platform first, mission growth second

先标准平台，再任务扩展

Step 01 步骤 01

Choose the closest standard platform by depth, access space and motion requirement.

按水深、进入空间与运动需求选择最接近的标准平台。

Step 02 步骤 02

Add sensing, lighting, sampling or support frames only when the mission requires them.

仅在任务需要时增加感知、照明、采样或支撑结构。

Step 03 步骤 03

Align purchase, rental or mission support with the project cycle.

按项目周期匹配采购、租赁或任务支持方式。
