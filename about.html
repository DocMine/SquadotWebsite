About Squadot Tec.

关于 Squadot Tec.

An engineering team built to
deliver underwater systems

一支具备产品化能力的
技术型水下机器人团队

Squadot Tec. should be understood as a productization-oriented engineering team working across underwater observation, structural inspection, constrained-space access, shallow-water checks and task-specific payload integration.

Squadot Tec. 应被理解为一支面向产品化交付的工程团队，围绕水下观测、结构巡检、受限空间进入、浅水摸查和任务挂载集成展开工作。

Identity

身份定位

The company is not a one-off prototype shop or internal lab page

这不是一次性原型工作室，也不是内部实验室页面

Identity 公司身份

A productization-oriented engineering team for underwater robotics platforms and mission support.

一支具备产品化能力的水下机器人平台与任务支持工程团队。

Current offering 当前提供

Micro to medium platforms, field support, rental, purchase and payload-oriented project expansion.

微型到中型平台、现场支持、租赁、采购与项目化挂载增配。

Scene focus 场景重点

Dams, hydraulic structures, nearshore assets, ponds, shafts, culverts and constrained industrial water spaces.

坝体、水工结构、近岸设施、池体、井室、涵洞和狭窄工业水下空间。

Delivery logic 交付逻辑

Start from a standard platform, then expand by payload, support frame and delivery model as needed.

先从标准平台进入，再围绕挂载、支架和交付方式进行按需扩展。

Team Background

团队背景

Platform, sensing, control and delivery experience in one team picture

在同一张团队能力图谱中同时看到平台、感知、控制与交付经验

Technical lead

技术带头

Current materials point to acoustics and underwater robotics R&D background with work spanning navigation, SLAM and underwater system design.

现有资料显示，团队具备声学与水下机器人研发背景，覆盖导航、SLAM 与水下系统设计方向。

Platform founder logic

平台创始能力

The capability picture includes ROV/AUV structure design, watertight components, control systems and field-side deployment logic.

能力图谱涵盖 ROV/AUV 结构设计、水密部件、控制系统与现场部署逻辑。

Perception and navigation

感知与导航

Available materials cover sonar SLAM, path planning, image enhancement and multi-sensor fusion directions.

现有资料覆盖声呐 SLAM、路径规划、图像增强和多传感器融合方向。

System and execution

系统与执行

Software, integration, dashboard, monitoring and field-oriented execution all belong to the current capability picture.

软件、系统集成、地面站监控与面向现场的执行支持都属于当前团队能力的一部分。

Company Approach

工作方式

Use standard platforms first, then grow toward the mission

先用标准平台进入，再围绕任务向外扩展

Step 01 步骤 01

Choose the closest standard platform by depth, space and motion requirement.

先按水深、空间与运动要求选择最接近的标准平台。

Step 02 步骤 02

Add sensing, lighting, sampling or support-frame logic only where the task actually needs it.

仅在任务真正需要时再增加感知、照明、取样或支架逻辑。

Step 03 步骤 03

Align purchase, rental or mission support mode to the actual project cycle.

再根据项目周期决定采购、租赁还是任务支持模式。
